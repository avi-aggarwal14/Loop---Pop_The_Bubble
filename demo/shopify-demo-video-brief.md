# Synthetic Shopify Demo Story

Use this for the demo video. The data is fictional but shaped like what Synapse
will pull from Shopify.

## Store

Luma & Lane is a DTC skincare brand. The fake Shopify store is:

```text
luma-lane-demo.myshopify.com
```

## The Raw Data Story

Week of 1 June 2026:

- Revenue: GBP 18.4k, up 39% week over week.
- Orders: 312, up 37% week over week.
- Conversion rate: 2.47%, up from 2.05%.
- New customers: 196, up 49%.
- Sessions: 12,640, up 14%.

Channel read:

- TikTok organic jumped from 12% to 31% of new customers.
- Instagram Reels is still working at 26% of new customers.
- Email is strong for returning buyers but not driving discovery.
- Meta ads spent GBP 520 and only produced GBP 440 in revenue.

Product read:

- GlowPatch Acne Dots are the breakout product.
- GlowPatch sold 790 units this week, up from 310 last week.
- GlowPatch produced GBP 7.1k revenue.
- GlowPatch has 410 units left, around 0.5 weeks of stock.
- Mini Travel Trio has 510 units in stock and zero sales.
- Weekend Reset Kit is slowing down and has 16+ weeks of stock.

## Growth Brief For The Video

Headline numbers:

```text
Revenue: GBP 18.4k, up 39% WoW
Orders: 312, up 37% WoW
Conversion: 2.47%, up from 2.05%
```

What's working:

```text
TikTok organic created the breakout. It jumped from 12% to 31% of new customers
and drove GBP 5.9k in revenue. GlowPatch Acne Dots are carrying the week:
790 units sold, GBP 7.1k revenue, and most high-volume orders came from TikTok.
```

What to cut:

```text
Meta ads are the clean cut. They spent GBP 520 for GBP 440 revenue, only 7
orders, and just 3% of new customers. Keep that budget away from Meta until the
TikTok stockout risk is handled.
```

Your one move this week:

```text
Reorder GlowPatch today and move Meta's GBP 520 into TikTok creator seeding.
```

Rationale:

```text
GlowPatch has about half a week of stock left while TikTok is producing the best
new-customer growth. If it sells out, the channel that just caused the revenue
spike has nothing to sell.
```
