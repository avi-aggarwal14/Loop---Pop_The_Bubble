# Synapse Demo — Voiceover Script

Narration to lay over the screen-recording flow `/ad/1 → /ad/2 → /ad/3 → /ad/4 → /ad/6`.
Voice direction: calm, confident founder. Conversational, not announcer. ~2.3–2.5 words/sec.

> **Pacing note:** the current recording is ~23s — too fast to read this and let each screen breathe. Re-record holding each screen for the **Hold** times below (total ≈ 48s). The compressed 23s version is at the bottom if you want to keep it short.

---

## Full script (~48s)

### Screen 1 — `/ad/1` "Ask before the move"
**On screen:** founder types *"I plan to decrease Red Bull Coconut & Berry sales next week"* → Synapse replies *"No. Increase it while demand is still compounding."*
**Hold ≈ 9s**

> "Every founder makes a call like this one. Coconut & Berry is already selling itself, so my instinct is to pull the ad spend off it and put that budget behind my slower drinks. Before I act, I ask Synapse. And it tells me: don't."

---

### Screen 2 — `/ad/2` Product hero → can zoom
**On screen:** the Coconut & Berry can, then it zooms into a white transition.
**Hold ≈ 4s**

> "Synapse connects straight to your Shopify store —"

---

### Screen 3 — `/ad/3` Product pull / "TikTok is carrying the breakout"
**On screen:** KPI cards (Revenue +28%, Units +42%), revenue-by-source breakdown, TikTok at 34%.
**Hold ≈ 10s**

> "— and pulls every product-level signal into one view. Revenue's up twenty-eight percent, units up forty-two. And one source — TikTok — is driving a third of it. This isn't noise. It's a real breakout."

---

### Screen 4 — `/ad/4` Full prediction / "Stockout likely inside the next order cycle"
**On screen:** stockout prediction, evidence cards, mubit recall of Tropical & Peach editions.
**Hold ≈ 13s**

> "Then it predicts what comes next. Coconut & Berry sells out in three to four days. And it's confident — because it remembers two past launches that broke the exact same way, and stocked out before the reorder landed. That's the difference: Synapse doesn't just see this week. It remembers every week."

---

### Screen 6 — `/ad/6` Final verdict / "Do not decrease Coconut & Berry"
**On screen:** verdict to increase the breakout, three moves, and the "decrease these instead" forecast.
**Hold ≈ 12s**

> "So the verdict overturns my plan. Don't cut it — push it while stock lasts, protect the reorder, and ease off the drinks memory says are about to fade. Ten products, one clear move. That's Synapse: your analytics, turned into one decision."

---

## Compressed version (~23s — fits the current cut)

- **Screen 1:** "Coconut & Berry is selling itself, so I want to move that ad budget onto my slower drinks. I ask Synapse first — and it says don't."
- **Screen 2 → 3:** "It pulls every Shopify signal: revenue up twenty-eight percent, and TikTok driving a third of it."
- **Screen 4:** "Then it predicts a stockout in three days — because it remembers two past launches that broke the same way."
- **Screen 6:** "The verdict: don't cut it, push it. Your analytics, turned into one decision."

---

## Delivery tips
- Land hard on the three hooks: **"it tells me: don't"**, **"it remembers every week"**, and the closing **"one decision."**
- Leave a half-second of silence on each screen change so the cut and the line breathe together.
- Record 2–3 takes of the closing line — it's the one people remember.
